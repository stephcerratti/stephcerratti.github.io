About

Hi! I’m Stephanie!

A front-end developer looking for my next big challenge. I come from a background in anthropology and experiential marketing, which I find gives me a fresh take in the tech industry. I unwittingly began my life of code as a 10 year old just trying to spice up my Neopets page. From there, I got re-introduced to the world of web development after some soul-searching living abroad in Australia. I happily learned that I was still just as passionate about it as my former ten-year-old self, and jumped in head first! I am a bootcamp graduate, but have learned a lot more on my own, too. I try to code a little every day, and am constantly learning new languages and improving my current ones. When I’m not writing code and working on projects, you will find me dancing away at a music festival or planning my next adventure.
